Social Programme

GeoMandu’s Social Programme features a Welcome Reception and Conference Dinner, fostering networking and celebration. Enjoy performances, canapés, and cocktails at venues. Find dates, dress codes, and costs, subscribe for updates, and contact organisers. Supported by partners, Nepal Geotechnical Society hosts.
