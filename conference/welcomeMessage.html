Welcome to GeoMandu 2026

Message from the Chair

Assoc. Prof. Dr. Mandip Subedi (Conference Chair)

On behalf of the Nepal Geotechnical Society, it is my great honor and pleasure to warmly welcome you to GeoMandu 2026 and to Kathmandu, at one of the most beautiful times of the year.

GeoMandu 2026 marks the third international conference in this series organized by the Nepal Geotechnical Society. Our journey began with GeoMandu 2023, the inaugural event, and was further strengthened by GeoMandu 2024, which successfully brought together more than 350 participants from over 25 countries. GeoMandu 2024 was a milestone in our effort to enhance international participation. Building on this progress, GeoMandu 2026 aims to further expand global collaboration and exchange in the field of geotechnical engineering.

Geotechnics continues to challenge us due to the often unpredictable behavior of soils, the complexity of ground conditions, and the growing demand for resilient infrastructure. While the fundamental theories may remain steady, rapid advancements in methodological approaches, experimental precision, numerical simulations, analytical techniques, and data processing capacity have significantly transformed our profession in recent years. Unfortunately, these advances have also widened the technological gap between developed and developing regions. To bridge this divide and foster accessibility, it is essential to convene on international platforms like GeoMandu, where we share innovations, knowledge, and experience.

Nepal Geotechnical Society. I take this opportunity to sincerely thank all participants, paper presenters, keynote and invited speakers, local collaborators, and our sponsors for making this conference possible.

Welcome once again to Kathmandu, and I wish you an engaging, insightful, and rewarding experience at GeoMandu 2026.

Message from the Convener

Dr. Dhundi Raj Pathak (Convener)

It is my great honor to serve as the Convener of the GeoMandu 2026 Committee and to extend a warm welcome to all participants.

As we commence the third international conference in the GeoMandu series, being held from November 25 to 28, 2026 in Kathmandu, we are excited to unite researchers, practitioners, and experts from around the world under the theme “Geotechnics for Mountain Infrastructure.” This theme highlights the unique challenges and opportunities of developing sustainable and resilient infrastructure in mountainous regions, where geohazards and complex geology demand innovative engineering solutions.

The conference provides a platform to exchange knowledge, explore emerging trends, and foster collaboration that bridges research and practice.

Welcome to Kathmandu, and I look forward to a productive and inspiring conference.

Message from the Convener

Er. Rajan KC (Youth Convener)

It is with great excitement and enthusiasm that I welcome you all to GeoMandu 2026, the third international conference of the Nepal Geotechnical Society, being held from November 25 to 28, 2026, in Kathmandu.

As a young geotechnical engineer, I feel truly honored to serve as the Youth Convener for this event. Our chosen theme, “Geotechnics for Mountain Infrastructure,” resonates deeply with the context of Nepal and other mountainous regions worldwide. The challenges of landslides, earthquakes, and fragile terrain call for innovative ideas, advanced technologies, and above all, the active involvement of the younger generation of engineers and researchers.

GeoMandu 2026 provides a unique platform where young professionals and students can engage directly with global experts, exchange fresh ideas, and contribute meaningfully to shaping the future of resilient and sustainable infrastructure. I strongly believe that the energy, creativity, and curiosity of youth, when combined with the wisdom and experience of senior experts, can bring powerful solutions to our geotechnical challenges.

On behalf of the youth community, I warmly welcome all participants and look forward to vibrant discussions, new collaborations, and inspiring exchanges throughout this event.

Thank you, and see you at GeoMandu 2026!
