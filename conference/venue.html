Venue

Radisson Hotel Kathmandu

The conference will be held at the Radisson Hotel in Kathmandu, a premier location offering state-of-the-art facilities and a welcoming atmosphere for all attendees. Conveniently located in the heart of the city, the Radisson provides modern amenities, comfortable accommodations, and renowned hospitality, ensuring a seamless and memorable experience for every participant. Surrounded by Kathmandu’s vibrant cultural sites, historic landmarks, and bustling markets, the venue offers a perfect blend of professional convenience and cultural richness. Easily accessible from major transit points, it not only provides an ideal setting for productive discussions and networking, but also allows guests to explore the unique heritage, cuisine, and warm hospitality that Nepal is known for. This harmonious combination of comfort, accessibility, and cultural immersion makes the Radisson Hotel an exceptional choice for hosting GeoMandu 2024.
