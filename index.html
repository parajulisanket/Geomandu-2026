About GeoMandu 2024

The Nepal Geotechnical Society extends a warm invitation to geotechnical engineers, geoscientists, professionals, and researchers from around the world to participate in “GeoMandu 2024,” the second installment of the esteemed “GeoMandu” series organized by the Nepal Geotechnical Society (NGS). This biennial event is scheduled to take place in Kathmandu on November 28th and 29th, 2024, with a central theme of “Geotechnics for Sustainable Infrastructure.”

Welcome letter from
Conference Chairman

Dear Esteemed Colleagues,

As Chairman of GeoMandu 2024, it is my pleasure to welcome you to Kathmandu for this landmark international conference. We look forward to insightful discussions, sharing of ideas, and forging new collaborations in the heart of Nepal.

GeoMandu 2024 will bring together geotechnical engineers, researchers, and professionals from around the globe. Our program features keynote addresses, technical sessions, workshops, and networking opportunities, all focused on advancing geotechnics for sustainable infrastructure.

We invite you to explore Nepal’s rich culture and heritage while engaging with leaders in the field. Your participation will contribute greatly to the success of this event and to the future of geotechnical engineering in our region.

With warm regards,

Prof. Netra Prakash Bhandary Chairman, GeoMandu 2024
